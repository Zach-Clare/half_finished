Domains work differently for mathematical and for computational polynomials. In mathematics, the domain is simply all numbers that can be plugged into your polynomial, which is pretty much any real number, it's an infinite range. In a computational setting, the domain is used to restrict the polynomial to help it describe one specific area, and seems to be used along with the window to help fits.
